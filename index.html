Welcome to my JS quiz!

JavaScript is one of the most popular programming languages!

Do you want to know how well you know JS?

To find out this, try this quiz!

  • The quiz consists of ten questions. Each time you attempt the quiz, the questions will vary.
  • To answer a question, simply click on one of the provided options.
  • If your answer is correct, the line will be highlighted in green. In case of an incorrect answer, two lines will be highlighted - the incorrect answer in red and the correct answer in green.
  • After completing all 10 questions, you will receive your result based on the number of correct answers.
  • To start over, click the "Start again".
  • You can enable sound by clicking on the icon in the upper right corner.
