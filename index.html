Welcome to my JS quiz!

JavaScript is one of the most popular programming languages!

Do you want to know how well you know JS?

To find out this, try this quiz!

  • The quiz consists of the ten questions. Each time you try, the questions will be different.
  • To answer a question, you just need to click on one of the answer options.
  • If your answer is correct - the line will be highlighted in green. If the answer is incorrect, 2 lines will be highlighted. The wrong answer is in red, and the correct answer is in green.
    • After answering the 10 questions you will get your result, depending on the number of correct answers.
  • To start over click on the button "Start again".
  • You can turn on the sound by clicking on the icon in the upper right corner.
